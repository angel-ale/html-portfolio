The Best Movies According to Angel

My Top Three Movies Right Now

Oppenheimer

What I loved most about this movie was the detail in the music and sound design. The sound was so crisp and almost tactile. The movie was an immersive experience.

Everything Everywhere All at Once

Where do I begin?!?! The layers on this film were beyond my expectations. The way the actors had to quickly adapt within even just the same scene was impressive. I have never seen a movie like this; wild is an understatement. It also has heartfelt themes of immigrant experiences, family, motherhood, identity, and mental health.

Suzume

This movie was visually stunning! It was a beautiful story and the plot kept me on the edge of my seat.
